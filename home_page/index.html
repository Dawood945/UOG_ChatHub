UOG ChatHub - Real Time Chat Application

UOG_ChatHub is a real-time messaging platform designed specifically for the University of Gujrat (UOG) to facilitate seamless communication among students, teachers, and university staff. It allows users to engage in individual or group conversations, share updates instantly, and stay connected within the campus community. With its user-friendly design and interactive features, UOG_ChatHub fosters collaboration, enhances connectivity, and supports a more engaged academic environment.
